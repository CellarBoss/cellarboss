# CellarBoss Mobile

CellarBoss Mobile lets you manage your wine cellar from your phone. It connects to your existing CellarBoss server and provides access to all your cellar data.

## What You Can Do

- **Browse your cellar** — View, search, sort, and filter your bottle collection
- **Add bottles and wines** — Create new records with a streamlined mobile form
- **Record tasting notes** — Capture tasting impressions with a rating and detailed notes
- **Manage reference data** — Add and edit winemakers, regions, countries, grapes, storages, and locations
- **View your dashboard** — See cellar statistics, charts, and activity at a glance

## Quick Start

1. [Install the app](/guide/installation) and connect to your CellarBoss server
2. [Log in](/guide/authentication) with your CellarBoss credentials
3. Browse your [cellar](/resources/bottles) from the Cellar tab
4. Add new [wines](/resources/wines) and [bottles](/resources/bottles) using the + button

## Navigation

The app uses a bottom tab bar for primary navigation. See the [Navigation](/guide/navigation) guide for details.

::: tip
For full documentation on shared concepts like wine types, drinking windows, and storage hierarchies, see the [Web documentation](https://docs.cellarboss.org/web/).
:::
