# Authentication

CellarBoss Mobile requires authentication to access your cellar data.

## Logging In

After connecting to your server, enter your email address and password, then tap **Sign In**.

![Login screen](/screenshots/auth-login.png)

::: info
Use the same credentials as the web application. For a fresh installation, the default administrator account is `admin@cellarboss.org` with password `adminpassword`.
:::

## Logging Out

To sign out, navigate to the **More** tab and tap **Sign Out** at the bottom of the menu.

## Session Management

Your session token is stored securely on the device. If your session expires, you will be returned to the login screen automatically.
