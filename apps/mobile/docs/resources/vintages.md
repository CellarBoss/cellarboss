# Vintages

A vintage represents a specific year's production of a [wine](/resources/wines). Vintages track the year and optional drinking window.

## List View

Vintages are accessed from a wine's detail screen, where all vintages for that wine are listed.

## Creating a Vintage

From a wine's detail screen, tap **Add Vintage**.

| Field       | Required | Description                                |
| ----------- | -------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| Year        | Yes      | The vintage year                           |
| Drink From  | No       | Year the wine becomes ready to drink       |
| Drink Until | No       | Year after which the wine is past its peak |

## Viewing a Vintage

Tap a vintage to see its full details, including associated [bottles](/resources/bottles) and [tasting notes](/resources/tasting-notes).

## Editing a Vintage

Tap **Edit** on the vintage detail screen to modify.

## Deleting a Vintage

Tap **Delete** on the detail screen. You will be asked to confirm.

::: warning
Deleting a vintage will also remove all associated bottles and tasting notes.
:::
