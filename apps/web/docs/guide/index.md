# CellarBoss User Guide

CellarBoss is a wine cellar management application that helps you track your wine collection, from purchase to consumption.

## What You Can Do

- **Track your bottles** — Record purchases, storage locations, and drinking windows
- **Manage your cellar** — Organise storage locations with a flexible hierarchy (rooms, racks, shelves)
- **Build a wine database** — Catalogue wines, winemakers, grape varieties, regions, and countries
- **Search and filter** — Find bottles quickly with full-text search, multi-select filters, and column sorting
- **Manage users** — Control access with admin and standard user roles

## Quick Start

1. [Log in](/guide/authentication) to your CellarBoss instance
2. Start by adding your [wines](/resources/wines) and [winemakers](/resources/winemakers)
3. Set up your [storage locations](/resources/storages) and [locations](/resources/locations)
4. Begin adding [bottles](/resources/bottles) to your cellar
