# Authentication

CellarBoss requires authentication to access the application. All pages except the login page are protected.

## Logging In

Navigate to your CellarBoss instance URL. If you are not already logged in, you will be redirected to the login page.

Enter your email address and password, then click **Sign In**.

::: info
For a fresh installation of CellarBoss, an administrator account will be automatically created with the username `admin@cellarboss.org` and password `adminpassword`
:::

![Login page](/screenshots/login.png)

## Logging Out

Click your profile icon in the top navigation bar and select **Log Out**. You will be redirected to the login page.

## Session Management

Your session is maintained via a secure cookie. Sessions expire after a period of inactivity. If your session expires, you will be redirected to the login page on your next action.
